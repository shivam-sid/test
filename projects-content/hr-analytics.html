PowerBI HR Analytics Dashboard

A PowerBI dashboard focused on HR analytics to enhance employee performance and reduce attrition through actionable data insights.

(Visualizations from the PowerBI HR Analytics Dashboard)

Project Objective:

Objective was to help an organization to improve employee performance and improve retention (reduce attrition) by creating a dashboard.

What is Attrition Rate?

Attrition rate is a metric that represents the percentage of employees who leave an organization over a specific period, either voluntarily or involuntarily.

It is a key indicator used by HR departments to monitor and improve their organization's workforce planning and overall management.

Methodology:

First I used #dataintegration , #datatransformation , #DataUnderstanding and #datacleaning to ensure the data was structured as needed. The data was in CSV format. I cleaned data using Power Query Editor. Next, I utilized a variety of #datavisualization , such as cards, slicers, pie charts, donut charts, and tree maps to demonstrate various visuals.
