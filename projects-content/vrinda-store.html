Project Overview:

Excited to share insights from my recent Excel Dashboard on Annual Sales Report. Here's a glimpse into the impactful work:

  • Topic: Annual Sales Report
  • Data set: Vrinda Store Data
  • Tools used: Excel

📝 Things I have Done:

  • Imported the Excel dataset of Vrinda store into Excel.
  • Removed unwanted columns.
  • Cleaned the data to handle missing values and outliers.
  • Created DAX Measures such as Age Group, Months, etc.
  • Built a dashboard with Line chart, Bar chart, Pie chart, Slicer, and other Filters.

Analysis Objective 🔍:

Vrinda store wants to create an annual sales report for 2022 so that they can understand their customers and grow more sales in 2023.

SAMPLE QUESTIONS EXPLORED:

  1. Compare the sales and orders using a single chart.
  2. Which month got the highest sales and orders?
  3. Who purchased more: men or women in 2022?
  4. What are different order statuses in 2022?
  5. List top 10 states contributing to the sales.
  6. Relation between age and gender based on the number of sales.
  7. Which channel is contributing to maximum sales?
  8. Highest selling category?

💡 Key Insights:

  • Women are more likely to buy as compared to men (64%).
  • Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh are the top 3 states (35%).
  • Adult age group (30-49) is max contributing (50%).
  • Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra channels are max contributing (80%).

📝 Final Conclusion to Improve Vrinda Store Sales:

Target WOMEN customers of age group (30-49) living in Maharashtra, Karnataka, Uttar Pradesh by showing ads/offers/coupons available on Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra.
