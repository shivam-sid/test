Project Overview:

  • Topic: Exploring Call Center Dynamics
  • Dataset: Call Center Metrics
  • Tools: CSV, PowerBI

🔍 What I've Accomplished:

  • Imported and cleaned raw call center data, ensuring accuracy and relevance.
  • Engineered key metrics including daily call volume, geographic distribution, top call reasons, sentiment analysis, and channel distribution.
  • Developed DAX measures for enhanced time analysis, enabling robust reporting capabilities.
  • Crafted a dynamic dashboard featuring an array of visualizations like column, map, tree map, donut, and bar charts, alongside interactive filters.

🔎 Analysis Objective:

Empowering Vrinda Store with actionable insights to fuel strategic decisions and drive sales growth in 2023 through a comprehensive annual sales report for 2022.

🔍 Sample Questions Explored:

  1. How does call volume fluctuate on a daily basis?
  2. Which states or regions contribute most to call traffic?
  3. What are the primary reasons behind customer calls?
  4. Which communication channels are preferred by customers?
  5. How do sentiments vary among customer interactions?
  6. How does call center performance compare across departments?

💡 Key Insights Unveiled:

  • Over 32.94K calls were logged, with an average duration of 25.02 minutes.
  • Friday emerged as the busiest day with 5.6K calls.
  • Neutral sentiments dominated the interactions, totaling 11K calls.
  • Billing inquiries ranked as the top reason for calls, comprising 23.46K instances.
